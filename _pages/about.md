I am currently postdoc at [LaMME](http://www.math-evry.cnrs.fr/doku.php#:~:text=Le%20laboratoire%20de%20Math%C3%A9matiques%20et%20Mod%C3%A9lisation%20d'%C3%89vry%20(LaMME)%20est%20une), Université d'Évry, working in the team of Mme. [Juhyun Park](http://www.math-evry.cnrs.fr/members/Jpark/welcome#:~:text=Juhyun%20Park.%20Ma%C3%AEtre%20de%20conf%C3%A9rence%20ENSIIE.%20ENSIIE.%20Laboratoire%20de%20Math%C3%A9matiques), funded by [FMJH](https://fondation-hadamard.fr/en/our-programs/transversal-programs/postdoc-fellowships/thematic-post-docs/).  

I was postdoc at [LMM](https://lmm.univ-lemans.fr/fr/index.html), Le Mans Université, working in the team of M. [Alexandre Brouste](http://perso.univ-lemans.fr/~abrouste/), M. [Laurent Denis](https://lmm.univ-lemans.fr/fr/le-laboratoire/les-membres-du-laboratoire/denis-laurent.html) and M. [Youssef Esstafa]() as part of the project [ANR EFFI](https://effi-stats.fr/), assigned to unit FR2962 Mathematical Research Federation of Pays de Loire, CNRS.

I was postdoc at LMRS, Université de Rouen Normandie, working in the team of M. [Mohamed Ben Alaya](http://mba.perso.math.cnrs.fr/) and M. [Serge Pergamenchtchikov](https://lmrs.univ-rouen.fr/fr/persopage/serguei-pergamenchtchikov) as part of the project MaSyComB, RIN Recherche (projet Tremplin) "Normandie Digitale". 

I was ATER at LAGA and [LAMA](https://www.u-pec.fr/fr/recherche/laboratoires/laboratoire-d-analyse-et-de-mathematiques-appliquees-lama-umr-8050), Université Sorbonne Paris Nord and Université Paris Est Créteil.

I was PhD student at [LAGA](https://www.math.univ-paris13.fr/laga/index.php/fr/), Université Sorbonne Paris Nord, under the supervision of M. Mohamed Ben Alaya and M. [Ahmed Kebaier](https://www.math.univ-paris13.fr/~kebaier/), funded by [FSMP](https://sciencesmaths-paris.fr/nos-programmes/doctorat).

I was Master student in the program Master Franco - Vietnamien de mathématiques appliquées [MFVMA](https://mfvma.hcmus.edu.vn/pages/about-us).
